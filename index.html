Hello! I am Christian Capezza, Assistant Professor (non-tenure-track) of Statistics for Experimental and Technological Research at the Department of Industrial Engineering at the University of Naples Federico II. I am member of the SFERe group. My work focuses on functional data analysis, statistical process monitoring, and generalized additive models. I teach Statistical Methods for Industrial Process Monitoring for the MSc in Mathematical Engineering and the MSc in Data Science. I enjoy using R for data analysis and visualization in my research.

Research interests:
  • Functional Data Analysis
  • Statistical Process Monitoring
  • Generalized Additive Models
  • Active Learning
